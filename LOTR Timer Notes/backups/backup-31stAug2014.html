How Many Times Can I Watch Lord Of The Rings?

It's very simple really. All you have to do is enter how many hours you have to spare, click on Begin, and the magical internet computery thing will tell you how many times you can watch The Lord of the Rings within the given time. Also, it will output which scene you'll be able to get up to, assuming that you don't enter an exact multiple of 39840 seconds (the total running time for the LOTR trilogy).
